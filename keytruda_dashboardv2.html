02 · Affordability
Cost as Multiple of Per Capita Income
One year of Keytruda equals how many years of average income?
The arithmetic of exclusion: In the US, Keytruda costs ~2.5× per capita income — expensive, but bridgeable with insurance. In India, it costs ~17×. Even with PMJAY, the patient still owes 15× their annual income out-of-pocket.
